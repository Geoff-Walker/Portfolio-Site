Features

Built for real use

Meal Planner & Shopping List
Plan the week. The app generates a consolidated shopping list across all planned meals — deduped, sorted, ready to use.
Cook History & Versioning
Every cook is recorded. Variations accumulate as a version history — promote any version to become the canonical recipe.
The Geoff Filter
A suggestion queue weighted by personal preference and how recently each recipe was cooked. Solves "what should I make?" without randomness.
Ratings & Ingredient Scaling
Per-cook ratings tied to the specific version made. Servings adjust in real time across all stages — built into the cook flow.
